What is the point of this game?

To test your knowledge of where the bars in Madison are located. There maybe be a few (or a lot) you didn't know existed, or weren't where you thought they were located.

Where can I see a list of the all bars included in the game?

You can see the full list here.

How is the location determined for each place?

I manually drop a pin on the map to add a new location.

I think a location is wrong, can you fix it?

Sure, just contact me and let me know.

How does a bar get added?

Right now this is mostly just places I have been to or have seen. If you don't see your favorite bar on here, contact me and I'll try and get it added.
